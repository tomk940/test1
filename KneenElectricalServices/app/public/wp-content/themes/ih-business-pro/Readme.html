Creative and Beautiful WordPress Theme with a Large Header

Description

IH Business pro is a very powerful and feature rich WordPress theme designed for Businesses and Corporates. With this theme you get access to tons of Social Icons, Layouts, Colors, Featured Sections and an awesome front page. Demo Here: http://demo.inkhive.com/ih-business-full-version/
